November 2022

Rocket league competition (1st place)

The design and construction of an Arduino UNO-based vehicle specifically tailored for competing in the Rocket League competition. The vehicle's body was meticulously fabricated using 3D printing techniques, utilizing acrylic material to ensure durability and a visually appealing design. The challenge was to create a high-performance vehicle capable of excelling in the fast-paced Rocket League environment. By integrating the Arduino UNO microcontroller as the central control unit, along with carefully selected sensors and advanced algorithms, the team achieved precise control, swift movements, and strategic decision-making. The 3D-printed acrylic body not only provided structural integrity but also enhanced agility and maneuverability.
